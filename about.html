Pacific Trails Resort

A New Experience Awaits

Escape to a one-of-a-kind experience in Northern California. Pacific Trails Resort offers a new experience for your whole family. Enjoy our luxury Yurts, which offer state of the art amenities. Take a stroll and do some bird watching through our 5 miles of luscious landscape or on beach. Feel like testing your skills on the Pacific Ocean? Grab one of our top of the line kayaks and plow your way through the white water to an amazing sunset over the Pacific. If you just feel like relaxing and want to unwind, we offer a variety of yoga classes to suit your experience level. Whether you are just looking for a weekend trip, or a long vacation, Pacific Trails Resort will make it an experience you will never forget.
